7 Leaves Cafe

7 Leaves Cafe

Be the change.

Focusing on Gandhi's philosophy, 7 Leaves Cafe strives on being the change that they wish in the world
through perfecting drinks and serving customers.

Locations: Artesia, Cerritos, Irvine, Torrance, 41 more cities

My Usual Order: Thai Tea with Honey Boba, EZ Ice, EZ Sweet

Wushiland Boba

Wushiland Boba

All the best from Taiwan

Coming from Taiwan, Wushiland Boba wishes to preserve their authentic taste. Hoping to spread the
Taiwanese taste, Wushiland Boba gurantees quality, consistency, and happiness.

Locations: Cerritos, Irvine, Fullerton, Westfield Century City, 29 more cities

My Usual Order: Milk Tea with Boba and Ice Cream, 50% Sweet, Less Ice

Cha for Tea

Cha for Tea

Preserving the legacy of their retail store in Taiwan, Cha for Tea aims to serve drinks that allow
the customer to savor the authentic flavor of Taiwan and fill their heart with joy.

Locations: Long Beach, Irvine

My Usual Order: Mango Green Tea with Boba, 2 Sweetner Pumps, Less Ice
